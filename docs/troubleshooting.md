# Troubleshooting

## The connector does not appear

1. Confirm your client supports remote HTTP MCP servers or custom connectors.
2. Confirm the endpoint is exactly `https://mcp.intelligentgrowth.app/mcp`.
3. Complete the browser sign-in.
4. Restart the client and check its connector list.
5. Start a new chat after the connector appears.

## Sign-in did not finish

Open the connection again and complete the browser flow. Do not paste an access token into a chat, issue or support message.

## The job is missing context

Ask the product to list the context it needs before beginning. Useful context normally includes the product, audience, alternatives, customer evidence, business constraint and deliverable.

## The result feels generic

Add the real page, interview language, constraints, evidence and decision you need to make. Then ask for one recommendation, the reasoning, assumptions and missing proof.

## Asking for help

Include:

- The AI client.
- The setup step that failed.
- The visible error category, with secrets removed.

Do not include:

- Access tokens or credentials.
- Private company content.
- Customer records.
- Confidential prompts or outputs.
