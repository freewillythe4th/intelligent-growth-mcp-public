# Example jobs

The strongest result starts with the current page, evidence and constraint, plus a clear decision or deliverable.

## Competitive gap

Bring:

- Your product or homepage.
- Three to five competitor pages.
- The buyer and use case.
- Customer evidence and a business constraint.

Ask:

```text
Use Intelligent Growth to run a competitive gap analysis. Ask for the missing context first. Then identify repeated claims, gaps and one opening worth using in the next positioning decision. Explain the reasoning and flag what still needs proof.
```

Expect:

- Repeated category patterns.
- Gaps and weakly owned claims.
- A recommended opening.
- Assumptions and missing evidence.

## Positioning review

Bring:

- The current homepage or positioning.
- Audience and use case.
- Alternatives buyers consider.
- Customer evidence and business goal.

Ask:

```text
Use Intelligent Growth to review my positioning. Ask for the missing context first. Recommend one direction, explain why it is stronger than the alternatives and separate evidence from assumptions.
```

Expect:

- A recommended market frame.
- A sharper differentiated value.
- Reasoning tied to the context.
- Proof gaps to resolve.

## Messaging review

Bring:

- The offer and audience.
- Interview notes or customer language.
- Current message.
- Proof and objections.

Ask:

```text
Use Intelligent Growth to sharpen my messaging. Ask for the missing context first. Build a clear hierarchy, show what should be said first and flag any claim that outruns the proof.
```

Expect:

- A messaging hierarchy.
- Value propositions and support points.
- Proof and objection gaps.
- A usable first draft to edit.

## Launch brief

Bring:

- The product change and audience.
- Deadline and business goal.
- Customer evidence and readiness.
- Channels, owners and constraints.

Ask:

```text
Use Intelligent Growth to plan this launch. Ask for the missing context first. Recommend the launch call, then build the brief, stakeholder actions and measures that follow from it.
```

Expect:

- A recommended launch call.
- Narrative and launch brief.
- Channel and stakeholder actions.
- Measures tied to the launch goal.
