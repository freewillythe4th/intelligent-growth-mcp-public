# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Do not open a public issue for a suspected vulnerability.

Use the private contact route at [intelligentgrowth.app](https://intelligentgrowth.app/) and include only the minimum information needed to reproduce the problem.

Do not include real credentials, customer data or confidential company content. Use a test account and redacted examples where possible.

## In scope

- Authentication or entitlement bypass.
- Exposure of protected service implementation.
- Exposure of another user's context or result.
- Token, redirect or connector security problems.
- Prompt-driven extraction that returns protected method definitions.

## Safe testing

- Use your own account and data.
- Keep requests bounded.
- Do not degrade service availability.
- Do not access another person's data.
- Stop and report if private data appears.
